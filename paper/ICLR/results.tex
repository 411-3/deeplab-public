\section{Experimental Evaluation}

{\bf{Weighted loss}} On the validation set. Without weighted loss: Pixelwise accuracy $90.13\%$, Classwise accuracy $73.68\%$, PixelIOU $59.80\%$. With weighted loss: Pixelwise accuracy $66.82\%$, Classwise accuracy $88.81\%$, PixelIOU $41.77\%$.
